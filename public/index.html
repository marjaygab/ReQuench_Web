A DLSL Water Vending Machine

ABOUT

ReQuench: A DLSL Water Vending Machine is a hot and cold water dispenser that uses RFID to identify its users. It aims to give the students and La Sallian Partners access to clean and safe filtered water inside the school.

It is being supplied by water gallons from Quenchers, a water filtration and distillation facility of De La Salle Lipa, to provide another option for Students and Partners to have their water containers filled.
